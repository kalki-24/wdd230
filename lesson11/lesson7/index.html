Today's Forecast

Today's weather forecast indicates Preston will be covered with fog.

We've received an alert that there will be dense fog in effect until 3 am MST Saturday. Mostly it will be around Highway 20 north of St. Anthony including Island Park and Henry`s Lake. The impact is hazardous driving conditions due to low visibility. Icy roads from patchy freeing drizzle is possible as well.

If driving, slow down, use your headlights, and leave plenty of distance ahead of you.
