Forcast Site Plan

Site Name: Forecast of the Day

Domain Name: forecastoftheday.com

Purpose

The purpose of Forecast of the Day is to provide a daily and weekly weather update for people within Idaho. Along with the weather forecast, the site will also provide daily news for the website users. The website will keep the users updated.
